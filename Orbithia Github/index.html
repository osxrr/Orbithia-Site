About the World

Orbithia is a world of drifting continents and powerful Moon Fragments. Airships rule the skies and dungeons hide rare resources. Your progression is strategic: pick a route, stock supplies, and manage risk.

NFT Collections

Main Collection — Airships (10,000)

  • Your airship is your base, with 4 character slots by default.
  • Rarity influences fuel efficiency, storage, and extra slots.
  • New players can buy a basic airship to enter.

Genesis — Exclusive Heroes (99)

  • Ultra-rare heroes with higher growth potential.
  • Prestige assets and long-term collectables.

Characters — The Core Commodity

  • Acquired via free starter mint, Drift Crates/Black Market, or events.
  • Hard cap: Level 5; rarity gates stat growth.
  • Stats: STR, MGK, HP, AGILITY, SPD. Two equipment slots by default.

Banners and skins can unlock via play or drops; some are airship-only, others character-only.

Core Gameplay

Homescreen → Map

Choose a destination. The game estimates travel and clear times, and whether your current loadout is likely to succeed.

  • Key metrics: Fuel, Storage, Slots
  • Supplies: Potions, one-time buff scrolls, keys

Dungeons & Fragments

Tackle dungeons using selected characters and supplies. Earn resources, loot, and Moon Fragments — the premium currency used for upgrades.

  • Airship levelling: travel farther, carry more, unlock extra slots
  • Character levelling: rarity-based stat gains (+1 to +5 per level)
