FAQ

Do I need NFTs to play?

New players can start with a basic airship; collecting heroes accelerates progression.

What are Moon Fragments?

Premium currency earned from dungeons, used to upgrade ships and heroes.

How do stats & levels work?

Heroes cap at Level 5; rarity gates growth across STR, MGK, HP, AGI, SPD. Each level adds incremental stats.

Can I mint on mobile?

Yes. We’ll support wallet connect and gas-abstracted checkout at launch.
