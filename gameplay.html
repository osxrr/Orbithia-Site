Gameplay Loop

  • Plan: Choose heroes, potions, one-time buff scrolls; manage fuel & storage.
  • Run: Travel time & success odds depend on loadout, rarity and supplies.
  • Reward: Loot & Moon Fragments used for upgrades.

Progression

  • Airships: go further, carry more, unlock slots.
  • Heroes: Level cap 5; rarity-gated stat growth (STR, MGK, HP, AGI, SPD).
  • Equipment: 2 slots; simple modifiers (+HP, +STR etc.).
