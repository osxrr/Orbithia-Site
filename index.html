FAQ

Do I need an NFT to start?

New players can enter with a basic airship; collecting heroes accelerates progression.

What are Moon Fragments?

Premium currency earned via dungeons, used to upgrade ships and heroes.

How do stats & levels work?

Heroes cap at Level 5; rarity gates total growth across STR, MGK, HP, AGI, SPD.
