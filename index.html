About Orbithia

Orbithia is a Web3 strategy RPG set in a world of moving continents and fractured moons. Players command customizable airships, recruit unique NFT heroes, and tackle resource-based dungeon challenges.

NFT Collections

  • Main Collection: 10,000 Airships, each with 4 character slots.
  • Genesis Heroes: 99 exclusive NFTs with unique stats and rarity.
  • In-game Unlocks: Banner rewards, loot crates, and transferrable NFTs.

Gameplay

Plan your expeditions carefully — select characters, stock up on supplies, and venture into dungeons. Dungeon clearing takes time and resources, making strategy and preparation essential.

Roadmap

  1. Phase 1: Genesis NFT launch (Airships + Heroes).
  2. Phase 2: Whitepaper release & dungeon prototype.
  3. Phase 3: Marketplace integration and community town hubs.
  4. Phase 4: Full Orbithia game release.
