Orbithia Whitepaper

The vision, mechanics, and economy of the Orbithia NFT Game Project.

Main NFT Collection

Airships (10,000) are the primary NFTs with 4 slots for characters.

Genesis Collection

Exclusive heroes (99) with unique stats and rarity.

Game Loop

From the home screen, players explore, choose dungeons, allocate resources, and use Moon Fragments to upgrade airships and characters.

Economy

Moon Fragments act as premium currency. Characters and airships have rarity and upgrade caps. Key metrics: fuel, storage, slots.
